Refund Policy

ProfitPilot is operated by Stonevia Commerce. This policy explains how refunds are handled and how to request one.

Overview
How refunds work
Payments for ProfitPilot are processed by our payment provider. Refund requests are handled according to the provider’s checkout process and applicable consumer protection laws.
How to request a refund
If you believe you are entitled to a refund:
  • Use the “receipt email” from your payment provider (recommended). It typically includes a self-serve help/refund link.
  • Or email us at support@ukwebtools.com with your purchase email and any receipt/order reference you have.
Eligibility & fair use
We review refund requests fairly and in line with applicable law. We also reserve the right to refuse refunds in cases of:
  • Misuse, abuse, or fraudulent activity
  • Excessive or repeated refund requests
  • Chargeback abuse
Important: ProfitPilot is a digital tool. Outputs are estimates based on the inputs you provide. It is not financial, tax, or legal advice, and we do not guarantee profit outcomes.
