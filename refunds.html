Refund Policy

ProfitPilot is operated by Stonevia Commerce. This page explains how refunds are handled and how to request one.

Overview
How refunds work
Payments for ProfitPilot are processed by Paddle, our Merchant of Record. Refunds are handled in accordance with Paddle’s checkout process, Paddle’s buyer terms, and applicable consumer protection laws.
Refund window
If you are purchasing as a consumer, you may request a refund within 14 days of purchase (the day after the transaction is completed). Refund requests are reviewed and processed by Paddle in line with their buyer terms and applicable law.
How to request a refund
To request a refund:
  • Use your Paddle receipt email (recommended). It typically includes a secure self-service help/refund link.
  • Or email us at support@ukwebtools.com with your purchase email address and any receipt/order reference you have.
Important: ProfitPilot is a digital estimation tool. Outputs depend on your inputs and assumptions. It is not financial, tax, or legal advice, and we do not guarantee profit outcomes.
