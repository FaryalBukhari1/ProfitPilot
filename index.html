FAQ

Is this affiliated with eBay/Etsy/Shopify?

No. ProfitPilot is an independent tool. Platform names (if shown) are for reference only; no endorsement or affiliation is claimed.

Does ProfitPilot use live currency exchange rates?

No live FX. You can optionally enter your own exchange rate inside the app for quick conversions. This keeps the tool simple and avoids inaccurate “live rate” promises.

What happens after the 5-day trial ends?

You’ll be asked to unlock PRO (one-time purchase) to continue. Your account remains; you can unlock later anytime.

How does PRO unlock work?

After purchase, Gumroad emails a license key instantly. You paste it inside the app to unlock PRO features.

Are the results guaranteed?

No. Outputs are estimates based on your inputs and assumptions (fees, returns, ads). Use it as decision support, not as a guarantee of profit.
