Frequently Asked Questions

General

Is ProfitPilot affiliated with eBay, Etsy, Amazon or any marketplace?
No. ProfitPilot is an independent profit and pricing calculator. Marketplace names are shown only for user reference.

Is this financial or legal advice?
No. All outputs are estimates based on your inputs. Estimates are for decision-making purposes only — consult professionals if needed.

Why do I need to log in?
Login links your trial and PRO status to your account and prevents abuse. You can use some tools without logging in, but PRO features require it.

Free Trial & PRO

How does the free trial work?
You get a 5-day trial period. During trial some advanced features (e.g., reverse pricing, competitor mode, bulk) are limited.

Is PRO a subscription?
No. PRO is a one-time purchase that unlocks unlimited access to all advanced features.

How do I unlock PRO after purchase?
After purchase, PRO access is automatically granted to your logged-in account. If you switch devices or log out, simply log back in — the app will recognise your PRO status.
