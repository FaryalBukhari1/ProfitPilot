FAQ

Clear answers. No fluff.

Is ProfitPilot affiliated with eBay/Etsy/Amazon/any platform?

No. ProfitPilot is an independent tool. Platform names may be used only as user reference.

Is this financial or legal advice?

No. Outputs are estimates based on your inputs. Use your judgement and consult professionals if needed.

How does the free trial work?

You get 5 days of access. Some advanced features are limited during trial (for example reverse pricing and competitor runs).

Is PRO a subscription?

No. PRO is a one-time purchase for unlimited access (as listed on the sales page).

How do I unlock PRO after purchase?

After purchase, Gumroad sends your license key by email. Paste it into the app unlock section to activate PRO.

Why do you require login?

Login is used to keep your trial period and PRO access linked to your account and to reduce abuse.

Can I see reviews without logging in?

Yes. The Reviews page is public. Login is only required to submit a review.
